Dining Halls at The University of Michigan

Welcome to University of Michigan Dining, where culinary excellence meets community spirit. Our dining services are designed to nourish and delight all students of the campus community. Our menus are thoughtfully curated to feature multiple cuisines from around the world, ensuring that every meal is an opportunity to explore new flavors and enjoy your favorites. From comforting classics to adventurous international dishes, our dining halls and cafés have something for every palate.Understanding the importance of safety and health, M Dining takes meticulous care to accommodate students with dietary restrictions and allergies. We offer a variety of meal choices that are clearly labeled with allergen information, and our team works closely with students to provide personalized support. Whether you need gluten-free options, nut-free zones, or have any other dietary needs, we’ve got you covered. Your well-being is our priority.

Locations

Our dining halls cover multiple classroom buildings, residencies and both, the central and north campus. Check out the all the locations:

  • Bursley

    Think of Bursley as 8 mini-restaurants: Signature for chef’s specials, 24 Carrots for vegan/vegetarian options, Halal for composed Halal entrees, Pizziti for pizza, Wild Fire for grilled favorites, Two Oceans for stir fry, Deli for made to order sandwiches, and Finale for desserts!

  • East Quad

    Featuring local, farm-to-table recipes and a wide variety of vegan and vegetarian options, East Quad is the place to be for the sustainability-minded among us.

  • Markley

    Markley students love having a dining hall where they live, especially during the winter months. This full-service dining hall lets you customize your own personal pan pizza, have your burger just the way you like it, and build your own toasted sandwich.

  • North Quad

    With its chandeliers and dramatically vaulted ceiling, North Quad is the classy place to dine on campus.

Nutrition

From international cuisine to vegetarian entrées to gourmet pizzas, dining at Michigan is all about options. With many dining halls, residential cafés and meal plans to choose from, there’s always a tasty way to satisfy your craving for something fresh, healthy, hearty, spicy, crunchy, juicy, grilled or made to order.

Meal Plans

An MDining meal plan is your ticket to what may well be the best collegiate dining in the nation. (See About Us to learn why.) There are several plans to choose from, all designed to meet the needs of U-M students. Hearty eater? There’s a plan for you. All-day snacker? We’ve got you covered. If you have questions about meal plans, let’s talk.

  • Change or Add A Meal Plan

    Students wishing to upgrade their winter term selected meal plan or add an optional meal plan can do by emailing mealplans@umich.edu.

  • Get Blue Bucks

    Blue Bucks are THE campus currency at U-M. Purchase them anytime and use them at virtually any on-campus café, market, grill, or dining hall. A convenient and easy-to-use alternative to cash.
